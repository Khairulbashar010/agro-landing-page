Growth Information

Growth Type: Kampung chickens are slower-growing than commercial broilers.
Rearing Period: They usually reach market weight (1.3 – 1.8 kg live weight) in about 90 – 120 days (3–4 months).
Quality: The longer rearing period gives them firmer meat and better flavour — ideal for the premium local market.

Selling Price

Expected Mortality Rate

Around 15 – 20% is typical for small-scale or semi-free-range kampung chicken farms, since they're less intensively managed than broilers.
