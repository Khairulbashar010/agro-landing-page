Sheep Qurban Project

6-month investment with 50/50 cost and profit sharing. Ideal for seasonal investment opportunities with transparent terms.

Contract Duration

Six months

Initial Cost Sharing (per sheep)

Profit & Cost Sharing

Cost Shared: 50/50
Profit Shared: 50/50

Selling Price

Average market price: RM 900-1,100 for ~30kg sheep

Age & Weight Specifications

Ideal Purchase Age: Aim to buy sheep that are already close to the sale/kurban weight to minimise risk and feeding time. In Malaysia sheep mature at 6-9 months for general maturity.
Target Weight: If target weight is ~30 kg for sale, you'd likely purchase a sheep maybe 6-9 months old with a live weight maybe 20-30 kg (depending breed). This ensures that within the six-month period the sheep can reach the sale weight.

Expected Mortality Rate

While your target is 5-10%, shorter intensive period projects may see lower or higher depending on management; use the 5-10% figure as a guideline.
